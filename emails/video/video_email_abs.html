Adam Costello : Video in Email Test
There are 3 scenarios:
• video plays in email client
• fallback image #1 displays, links to video file
• fallback image #2 as static image
 
 This is by no means a comprehensive test, just a proof of concept that videos can be embedded in emails. Email clients have varying degrees of support, so robust testing and acceptable user experience should be implemented.
 
 
If your email client does not allow video tags, you'll see one of two fallback images.
